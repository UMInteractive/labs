Laser Cutting

UM School of Architecture Thomas P. Murphy Design Studio Building

The UM SoA has 4 laser cutting machines available for UM SoC students to use strictly for school projects.

Materials

  • MDF
    • The UM SoA sells 1/8 in MDF sheets
    • 24in by 36in - $2.00 Sheet
    • 18in by 32in - $1.00 Sheet
    • Misc ~ 17in by 18in - $0.50 Sheet
    • Misc ~ 12in by 32in - $0.50 Sheet
  • Any other materials have to be approved by UM SoA:
    • Solid Wood
    • Acrylic - Must have proof

Materials to avoid

  • PVC (Poly Vinyl Chloride)/vinyl/pleather/artificial leather
  • Thick ( >1mm ) Polycarbonate/Lexan
  • ABS
  • HDPE/milk bottle plastic
  • PolyStyrene Foam
  • PolyPropylene Foam
  • Epoxy
  • Fiberglass
  • Coated Carbon Fiber
  • Anything Edible
  • Material with Sticky Glue Backing
