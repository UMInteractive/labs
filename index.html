XR Lab

Old Uplink Studio, Across from Learning Center

The XR Lab is a dedicated space for students to design and develop immersive media, including digital games, 360 videos, and virtual reality experiences. The lab will be equipped with high end computers setup with the latest in virtual reality and 360 stitching technology. A permanent open area will be available to develop room scale experiences, run motion capture sessions, and play test prototypes. Additionally, the lab will have audio recording equipment to record high quality voice over. The lab will allow teams of students a hands on experience with the entire production process of creating immersive media.
