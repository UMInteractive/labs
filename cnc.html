CNC @ UM Emerging Media Lab

Wolfson, 1st Floor, Room 1018

In the lab, we have the Nomad 883 Pro. This device allows you to mill and cut various material. Unlike 3D printing, CNC (Computer Numeric Control) Milling, is a subtractive process that removes from your stock material. It is generally, faster then 3D printing.

  • Plastics

    • HDPE
  • Metal

  • PCB/Copper
